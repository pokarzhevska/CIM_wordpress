FAQ
01
What types of documents can be processed using CIM ONLINE'S software?

1.Cim online processes incoming invoices at the header and line level. Sales orders are received as PDF documents. Order confirmations are extracted at the line level, enabling the ERP system to compare the purchase order with the order confirmation."

02
Can the software be customized to meet my organization's specific needs?

2. The software can be configured in such a way that each customer can process their own documents. It's just a matter of configuration. Software modifications or customizations are not required."

03
What are the benefits of using CIM ONLINE'S AI-powered document processing software?

3. "The benefits of CIM Online are numerous. To begin with, OCR (Optical Character Recognition) is currently used for all document recognition software. This has been sufficient for simple plain text for years. However, as developers of document recognition software, we found this to provide inadequate quality. Traditional OCR reading and interpretation errors still persist for every user. AI, computer vision, deep learning, and natural language programming are much better techniques. We have combined these methods to create a product with the best possible recognition. Whether it's line recognition or column recognition, these techniques allow us to eliminate the reading errors of OCR."

04
What kind of technical support does CIM ONLINE provide for its software?

4. "CIM Online is a cloud solution that operates on multiple servers in Europe. KC Netherlands B.V., the owner of this cloud software, ensures the most optimal security, and every data transfer is done through 2-way SSL APIs. This guarantees secure data transmission. Our task also includes monitoring the performance of these servers. Furthermore, we continue to develop our software based on customer requirements."

05
What security measures does CIM ONLINE have in place to protect my confidentail documents?

5. "In our view, every document is a confidential document, whether it's invoices, orders, or other types of documents. The only thing CIM Online does is analyze the document layout and identify where the values that need to be recognized are located on the document. These coordinates are stored in a database. Once the documents are processed, they are presented to the customer. CIM ONLINE does not retain any documents. The provision of data is done through 2-way SSL APIs."
